Hub - Building Connections on Campus

Fall '19

Contribution

Concept Development, Product Design, UI/UX, Visual Design

Overview

Hub is a mobile app that facilitates student engagement in campus organizations by enabling them to find, join, organize, and propose organizations in a central location.

Features include a recommendation engine to suggest groups that students are likely to join, as well as a system to propose new organizations that can alleviate the overhead effort required to start an on-campus organization.

Strategy

Currently, most student run organizations rely on Facebook or Group Messaging, which arenâ€™t tailored for organization leaders. By offering features that allow leaders to create events, send updates, assign tasks, and manage group members, Hub will create immense value for the people most involved in the student-organization ecosystem.

Once organization leaders join, the members of their groups will have to join, and after seeing the convenience of consolidating organization info, ask for the service in the other groups they participate in.
