Hubstream - Investigate with Intelligence

Summer '18

Contribution

UX Analysis + Research, Wireframing

Overview

Hubstream is a company based out of Seattle that offers case management software. What sets Hubstreams product apart from the others is it's ability to automatically link similar information across cases and jurisdictions.

As the Design Intern, I conducted a Heuristic Evaluation of the current product, led a Usability Test, and created design solutions informed by my research. After creating wireframes based on my solutions, I presented my work to mangement, the lead designer, and the sales team. The sales team specifically was excited that my design significantly improved the intuitiveness and navigability of my design.
