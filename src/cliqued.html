Cliqued - How's your taste?

Fall '19

Contribution

Concept Development, Product Design, UI/UX, Visual Design

Overview

Cliqued is a social, music experience that helps people connect with others through music.

Features include a timeline for users to keep up with what their friends are currently listening to, group messaging messaging where users can share music, collaborate on playlists, and track who has the best taste in music.

Strategy

Due to the lack of an intuitive solution for sharing music, my friends and I still rely on Facebook Messenger and iMessage to share songs and keep up with each others music interests. This is an obvious opportunity in the music streaming market which none of the current streaming platforms have capitalized on.

Active and passive interaction are both important for online social relationships, so in addition to direct peer-to-peer sharing, features that facilitate passive interactions such as a timeline, music curation, and music discovery in accordance with a user's connections are crucial.
